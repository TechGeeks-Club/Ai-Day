Call for Participation in: Hackathon at AI Day 2024

Are you passionate about artificial intelligence (AI) and brimming with innovative ideas? Here's your chance to showcase your creativity and entrepreneurial spirit at the AI Idea Competition, part of AI Day at the University of 8 May 1945 GUELMA!


We are happy to invite you to participate in this exciting hackathon, This 24-hour hackathon pits teams against each other in a battle of ingenuity. Tackle one of two captivating topics, one firmly rooted in AI,the other will challenge you to apply your AI knowledge to a broader theme..

Compete for 24 hours, tackling real-world challenges. Hone your technical skills, network with industry leaders, and potentially win life-changing prizes. This is your chance to make a mark on the future of AI.

when the hackaton ends , the participants will present their works to the juries who will judge and pick up the winners...

Here's why you should participate:

  • This hackatoon is a great opportunity to showcase your creativity and programming skills to a group of experts and investors.
  • A chance to build a network of relationships with new people from different fields..
  • Develop your technical skills, teamwork skills, and communication with team members.
  • And finally, valuable prizes await the high achievers.

How to participate:

  1. Create a team: Gather 3-5 participants for a collaborative effort.
  2. For the registration , you need to head over to our website (Join Now ) and confirm your participation.
  3. Shortlisted teams will receive detailed information via email, outlining the next steps.

Call for Participation: AI Best Idea Competition at AI Day 2024

Dear Students,

Are you passionate about artificial intelligence (AI) and brimming with innovative ideas? Here's your chance to showcase your creativity and entrepreneurial spirit at the AI Idea Competition, part of AI Day at the University of 8 May 1945 GUELMA!


We're excited to invite you to participate in this thrilling competition where you can pitch your AI-based ideas to a panel of esteemed judges and fellow students. Whether you have a groundbreaking concept or a visionary solution to a real-world problem, we want to hear from you!

Here's why you should participate:

  • Opportunity to present your AI idea to industry experts and potential investors.
  • Chance to receive valuable feedback and guidance from experienced mentors.
  • Networking opportunities with peers and professionals in the AI and startup ecosystem.
  • Prizes and recognition for the best ideas.

How to participate:

  1. Form a team or participate individually
  2. Develop a compelling AI idea addressing a specific challenge or opportunity.
  3. Prepare a concise pitch presentation outlining your idea, its potential impact, and your implementation plan. (In video format or PPT format)
  4. Submit your entry. At the following link: click_here
  5. End the video format or PPT format if you have one to the following email: ai.day2024@univ-guelma.dz
  6. Selected teams/individuals will be invited to pitch their ideas live during AI Day, where winners will be announced and awarded.
