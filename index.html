Call for Participation: AI Best Idea Competition at AI Day 2024

Dear Students,

Are you passionate about artificial intelligence (AI) and brimming with innovative ideas? Here's your chance to showcase your creativity and entrepreneurial spirit at the AI Idea Competition, part of AI Day at the University of 8 May 1945 GUELMA!


We're excited to invite you to participate in this thrilling competition where you can pitch your AI-based ideas to a panel of esteemed judges and fellow students. Whether you have a groundbreaking concept or a visionary solution to a real-world problem, we want to hear from you!

Here's why you should participate:

  • Opportunity to present your AI idea to industry experts and potential investors.
  • Chance to receive valuable feedback and guidance from experienced mentors.
  • Networking opportunities with peers and professionals in the AI and startup ecosystem.
  • Prizes and recognition for the best ideas.
